Why Kalory Help?

Three promises we'll never break.

100% Private

All your data lives on your device — nowhere else. No servers, no cloud, no databases. We physically cannot see your data because it never leaves your phone.

Zero Tracking

No analytics, no ads, no tracking pixels, no SDKs watching you. We don't know who you are, what you eat, or when you open the app — and we want to keep it that way.

Completely Free

No subscriptions, no paywalls, no in-app purchases. Every feature is available from day one. Nutrition tracking shouldn't cost you money or your data.
