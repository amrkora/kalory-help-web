Accessibility Statement

We are committed to making Kalory Help accessible to everyone.

Our Commitment

Kalory Help is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities.

What We Do

We strive to ensure that this website and the Kalory Help app are accessible by following best practices including: using semantic HTML, providing sufficient colour contrast, ensuring keyboard navigability, including descriptive alt text for images, and supporting screen readers.

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Kalory Help. If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us at contact@kalory.help. We take accessibility feedback seriously and will do our best to respond promptly.
