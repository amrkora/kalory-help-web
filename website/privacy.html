Privacy Policy

Your privacy is not just a feature — it's the foundation of Kalory Help.

Your Data Stays on Your Device

Kalory Help is a fully offline nutrition tracking app. All data you enter — meals, water intake, weight, and profile information — is stored exclusively on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. There are no user accounts, no cloud sync, and no remote databases. Your data exists only on your device.

No Analytics or Tracking

Kalory Help does not use any analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies. We do not track your usage patterns, location, or any behavioral data.

No Third-Party Sharing

Since no data ever leaves your device, there is nothing to share with third parties. Your nutrition data belongs to you and only you.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage in your device settings. Once deleted, data cannot be recovered — because we never had a copy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at contact@kalory.help.

About This Website

This website does not use cookies, analytics, or any third-party tracking. Fonts are self-hosted — no requests are made to external font services. No personal data is collected through this website.
