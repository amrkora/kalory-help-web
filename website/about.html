About Kalory Help

Free, private, and built for your device — not our servers.

Kalory Help is a completely free, privacy-first nutrition tracking app that works entirely on your device. Track meals, water intake, and weight — all without an account, internet connection, or handing over your personal data.


Most nutrition apps want your email, your location, and your eating habits — then sell that data to advertisers. We think that's wrong. Kalory Help stores everything locally, supports multiple languages, and will never ask for your personal information. No subscriptions, no ads, no hidden costs.
